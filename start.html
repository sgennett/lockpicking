Choosing a Practice Lock

Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced:
Start with a cheap Master Lock (readily available and consistently flawed), then move up to an ABUS 55/30 or Master Lock 140 (with a spool pin), and finally tackle an American 1100 (spools & serrated pins).

Lock ModelPriceDifficulty
Master Lock (e.g. No. 140) $ Easy
ABUS 55/30 $$ Medium (introduces security pins)
American 1100 $$$ Hard (spools & serrated pins)
